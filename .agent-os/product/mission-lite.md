# Product Mission (Lite)

Aston OSINT is a self-hosted intelligence pipeline that helps Aston's luxury brand teams profile Ultra High Net Worth prospects and screen AML/KYC compliance by aggregating five open-source data sources (OCCRP Aleph, OpenSanctions, ICIJ OffshoreLeaks, Pappers, GDELT) and synthesising results into structured intelligence briefs via Claude.

The pipeline serves ops staff and sales advisors handling EUR 20K-500K+ transactions. Users submit a name via a web form or API call and receive a PDF intelligence brief with risk scoring in under 90 seconds — replacing EUR 2K-5K per-report intelligence firms with a zero marginal cost solution.
