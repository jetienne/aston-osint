# Product Mission (Lite)

Aston OSINT is a self-hosted intelligence platform that helps Aston's luxury brand teams profile Ultra High Net Worth prospects, verify AML/KYC compliance, and generate sales intelligence through a chat-based query and report workflow, powered by SpiderFoot on dedicated EU infrastructure.

Aston OSINT serves sales advisors and compliance officers handling EUR 20K-500K+ luxury transactions with UHNW clients whose profiles are invisible to standard CRM tools. Unlike third-party intelligence firms at EUR 5K+ per report, Aston OSINT runs at fixed infrastructure cost — it exposes a simple async API (submit a target, poll for results) that any consumer can integrate with.
